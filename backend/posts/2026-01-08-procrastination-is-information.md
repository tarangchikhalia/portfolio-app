---
title: "Procrastination is information"
date: 2026-01-08
tags: [philosophy, meta]
excerpt: "I used to fight procrastination. Now I treat it as a signal that something about the task is mis-specified."
---

I used to fight procrastination as if it were a moral failure. Now I treat it as data. When I cannot bring myself to start a task, the task itself is usually the problem — under-specified, mis-scoped, or pointing in a direction I do not actually want to walk.

The reframe is small but it changes everything. Instead of pushing harder I ask: *what am I avoiding telling myself about this work?* The answer is almost always more useful than the work would have been.
